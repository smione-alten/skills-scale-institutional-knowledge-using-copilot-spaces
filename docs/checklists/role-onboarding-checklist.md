# Role Onboarding & Role-Clarity Checklist

Use this checklist when assigning a role for a project or when updating role documentation.

- [ ] Role entry created/updated in docs/octoacme-roles-and-personas.md
- [ ] Role Template filled out (docs/templates/role-persona-template.md)
- [ ] Stakeholders (PO, PM, Tech Lead) have reviewed responsibilities
- [ ] Decision authority and escalation path communicated
- [ ] Onboarding tasks assigned (access, tooling, calendars)
- [ ] Acceptance criteria for role met
- [ ] Link to role owner (person) and contact info added
